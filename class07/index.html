Irregular Migration to EU

  • Increased irregular migration to EU since 2014
  • Over 1 million people crossed Mediterranean to Europe during 2015; over 3,770 died trying
  • EU response has been mostly enforcement, with EU military operation in Southern Central Mediterranean and NATO operation in Eastern Mediterranean
  • In fall 2015 ESI proposes a deal to get Turkey to accept rapid return of all refugees attempting to enter Greece through irregular channels.
  • EU-Turkey deal announced in March 2016. Aim is to "break the business model of the smugglers" by reducing demand while also increasing costs through enforcement.
